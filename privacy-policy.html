Our Philosophy

nonoiam is built on a simple belief: your presence matters more than your data. We collect the absolute minimum information needed to provide you with a silent, peaceful social experience.

What We Collect

  • Anonymous User ID — A random identifier (UUID) created when you first open the app. It is not linked to your name, email, phone, or any personal information.
  • Your Choices — The color tone and writing style you select. These are stored to display your posts consistently.
  • Your Posts — The text and emotion you choose when you "leave" a post. Maximum 3 sentences, maximum 2 per day.
  • Silent Reactions — When you acknowledge someone's post ("I'm here", "I understand", etc.).

What We Do NOT Collect

  • Your real name
  • Your email address
  • Your phone number
  • Your IP address
  • Your location
  • Your device identifiers
  • Your contacts
  • Your browsing history
  • Your usage analytics beyond basic app functionality

How We Use Your Data

Your anonymous data is used only to:

  • Display your posts to other anonymous users in your emotional room
  • Match you with emotionally similar posts (our algorithm never uses popularity)
  • Show your personal emotion map (Premium feature, visible only to you)

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or trade your data with anyone. There are no advertisers, no analytics partners, no third-party trackers. Period.

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on Supabase servers with Row Level Security (RLS) enabled. All data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest. Your anonymous UUID is the only key — even we cannot link your posts to a real person.

Data Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from the Profile screen. When you delete your account, all your posts, reactions, and locked posts are permanently removed. This action is irreversible.

Children's Privacy

nonoiam is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Premium Features

Premium subscriptions are processed through Google Play. We do not handle or store any payment information. Premium features never boost visibility, promote posts, or alter feed priority. Money never buys attention on nonoiam.

Changes

If we update this policy, we will notify users within the app. Significant changes will require your acknowledgment.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Reach us at privacy@nonoiam.app
